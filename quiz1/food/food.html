My Hometown Local Food

1. Kue Rangi

Rangi cake is a cake made from grated coconut and starch, then printed in a special mold (like a cake mold but the holes are shallower).
The cake then grilled, and lastly covered in brown sugar sauce that is soft in the mouth.

2. Sayur Gabus Pucung

The main ingredient of the Pucung Gabus vegetable is fried snakehead fish, which is mixed with special spices which contains kluwek.
The presence of kluwek is what makes the Pucung Gabus Vegetable sauce black like rawon.
The taste of Sayur Gabus Pucung is quite tasty, and can make anyone who eats it addicted.

3. Bir Pletok

Pletok beer is a drink made from ginger, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and various other spices.
Despite its "beer" name, this drink does not contain alcohol at all.
In fact Pletok Beer is very good for the health of the body, and has the power to treat diseases.
The word "beer" itself, is taken from Arabic which means a well or a water source.
