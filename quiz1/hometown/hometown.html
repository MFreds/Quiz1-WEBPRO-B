About My Hometown

My hometown is in Bekasi, West java.
Located in the eastern border of Jakarta.
It serves as a commuter city within the Jakarta metropolitan area, although it has notable trade, business and processing industries.
Bekasi is one of the oldest cities in Indonesia, and has a history of being the capital city of the Kingdom of Tarumanagara.
At that time, the name of Bekasi was Dayeuh Sundasembawa.
As a dormitory city, many middle-up satellite areas have been developed in Bekasi, complete with their own shopping malls, schools, hospitals, club houses, water park, and shuttle bus services to central Jakarta.
