My Hometown Tourist Spot

1. Alun alun Kota Bekasi

The atmosphere in this park is beautiful and cool. Because around this park there are many shady trees.
The facilities that are already available in this square park are free wifi, children's playgrounds, and jogging spots.

2. Curug Parigi

This waterfall has a very beautiful and beautiful panorama.
This waterfall has been used as a shooting location, one of the titles is "Sang Penakluk", played by Suzanna.
In this place there are also great spots to make as your photo background.

3. Pantai Mekar

Apart from the clear water, this beach also has beautiful views.
If you are hungry, this place also sells food.
In the area around this Mekar beach, some of the residents work as fishermen.
So you can rent a boat to get around this blooming beach.
